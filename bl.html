Hooray for silly titles!! Its secondary title would be Ocean Sunset Eyes. It's not really swirly but that's not the point! This is an eyelook where I picked the warm shadows first and then wasn't sure what to do for an overall lid color... so I asked my significant other to pick a color for me! He ended up picking Fringe from Urban Decay's Electric Palette! I wasn't sure how it was gonna turn out but I am pleasantly surprised with the results. I think it's a nice combination of warm and cool tones. I have a product list at the very bottom of the post if you interested in what I used. You don't need to use these exact products for this, just use similar colors! 





Another thing I really like about this look is how it's still pretty visible even with glasses on!! While I appreciate more toned down makeup, I'm really into bright, fun colors. I definitely want to do a tutorial on this look so expect to see that in the future. If you're into false lashes, they would be pretty great with this look. I think lashes that were as thick/fluffy would be nice just because I think the standout of this look is the eyeshadow. If you recreate this look please let me know, I'd love to see it!!
