Lifeless, stoic, and stiff is what the world of fashion photography was before Richard Avedon stepped into the spotlight. Movement, intimacy, space, and form began to appear in fashion photography because of Avedon. New life rushed onto the photographic scene. Avedon's work is full of dynamic poses with strong sillhouettes. His work pushed the boundaries of what is considered fashion photography, opening new avenues for future photographers. Many of his photographs are considered to be emblematic and iconic. Evocative, emotive, exiciting all good words to describe his work. Avedon had a deep fascination with tragic beauty and was deeply inspired by his ill younger sister.
